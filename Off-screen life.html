Jennifer Lawrence's Life off set

Jennifer Lawrence, while a busy actress, finds herself working off screen with certain organizations. She has shown her support for, planned parenthood, or more formally known as,Planned Parenthood Federation of America a corporation that deals with Reproductive Health such as, STD prevention, Birth Control, and even abortions. Jennifer has shown that she fully supports the organization by participating in a June 2017 video that spoke out about defunding the organization. She also openly supports Feminism and the Body Positive movements. She has also lent her support for charitable programs such as, The World Food Programme and the Feeding America, and well as the Thirst Project. Another program she has advocated for is the DoSomething program, which tries to motivate young people to make positive changes online and offline. There are also other humanities work that Jennifer Lawrence has helped or collaborated with such as the Time's up initiative which sought out to protect women from harrassment/discrimination. She also went on to found the Excellent Cadaver production company.
